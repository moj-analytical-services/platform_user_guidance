Part 7 Security Operating Procedures

  • You must not put any sensitive (OFFICIAL) data into the platform without getting permission from Robin Linacre or get in contact on the #analytical-platform channel on Slack.
  • Do not rely on the platform for anything yet, especially for saving your work. We cannot guarantee that your data is safe and will not be lost.
  • If you are responsible for a team, ensure that users have the correct permissions.

7.1 General security guidance

  • Do not access the platform from your personal computer.
  • Keep passwords secure and do not share accounts with anyone else. You are responsible for the security of your passwords and you will be required to change it at frequent intervals.
